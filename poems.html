The Path to Nowhere

Train rides,
Slight highs,
Weekly.
School year ends,
Summer begins,
Yearly.
Big change,
The start of something new.
New,
Yet the same.
Repetition.
A horizontal line.
Go to college,
Get a job,
You're set.
Find a husband,
Raise a family,
Safe bet.
Your mom did it,
Your grandma did it,
And so will you.
Repeat the process,
Nothing new.
Buy a home,
Settle down,
Raise a few.
Vacation is the place for exploration,
Home is the place for cultivation.
Go to work,
Come home,
Lead by example.
Pick up the phone,
Do all your chores,
Lead by example.
But why?
Did my mom's mom's mom die the happiest person alive?
Take the world for that matter,
Stop at street corners and listen to all the chatter.
Turn on CNN,
Take out a piece of paper and a pen.
Step outside your home,
Just to feel that negative energy.
Turn on the TV just to hear another man has been shot and killed.
Start a happy journal just to feel your heart cry.
Okay we get it,
The worlds filled with hate,
But that's not the point that I'm trying to make.
The point is the world is moving in a horizontal line.
The point is most people now a days don't stop to take the time.
To think,
Explore their minds,
Repetition is a vicious cycle,
A cycle we must end.
Drop out of college,
Find your passion,
Maybe live with your best friend.

Creator of the Incubator

Tyler, The Creator
The producer,
The speed racer.
A jack of all trades
A misogynist,
Yet a gay.
Paradoxical,
Illogical,
Yet it makes so much sense.
Cover up your lies, disguise,
Then run into a fence.
Figuring out your life publically,
Musically,
Verbally with art,
Pen and paper in your hand,
First page,
It all starts
There has to be that one point,
That initial take off.
Vroom vroom,
I'm racing,
A speed racer inside these thoughts.
Tyler is a creator,
He made it out.
Me?
I'm an incubator,
I help others sprout.

The Minds Made of Matter

Turn on happiness with a switch.
Block out anxiety with a click.
Control your brain,
Keep your mind tamed.
Use up all your mental energy.
Yet stay awake.
